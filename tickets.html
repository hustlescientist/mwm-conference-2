Frequently Asked Questions

What if I can't attend after purchasing?

We offer full refunds up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person at no additional cost.

Are meals included with my ticket?

All tickets include lunch and light refreshments. VIP and Millionaire Circle tickets include additional premium catering options.

Can I upgrade my ticket later?

Yes! You can upgrade your ticket at any time before the event, subject to availability. You'll only pay the difference in price.

Is there parking available?

Cox Pavilion offers free parking for all attendees. VIP and Millionaire Circle ticket holders receive reserved parking spots.
